Parish Announcement and Notices

  • New Year wishes

1.Dear Christians, The Peace of Christ be with you and your families.The Parish Priest Fr. Ignacio Flores welcomes you all, one more time to our Parish and wishes you a year full of blessings.

  • PUBLIC HOLIDAYS:

2. Tommorrow, 2nd January 2023 will be the New Year holiday. We shall have Mass at 9:00am in our Parish and Olympic Sub Parish. kindly note that the Parish offices will be closed on this day.

  • RESUMING OF CATECHESIS CLASSES:

a)Catechesis classes for the Sacrament of Confirmation will resume on 3rd January 2023. Classes will start at 10:00am until 12 noon.
b)For the Sacrament of Baptism, Holy Communion and Profession of Faith step they will resume on 8th January 2023. Classes will start at 7:00am.
c)Catechumens should report to their respective regions where they do their catechesim. (Olympic and Adams region)

  • INFANT REGISTRATION:

4.Registration for infant Baptism from 5years and below will start from 3rd January 2023 until 7th January 2023. Requirements will be
(i) Original Birth Certificate or Notification form (ii) registration fee.

  • TITHING SUNDAY

5. Next Sunday will be our first tithing Sunday of the year and we are invited to share the first fruits of our labour to the Lord. Our tithing envelopes are available
at the Parish Office and besides the sadaka boxes. Thank you for your genorosity and God bless you abundantly.

  • SIGNING OF SCHOLARSHIP FORMS

6. Dear Christians for the signing of scholarship forms kindly present Baptismal card of the child and a copy of church due card of the parent or gurdian in the Parish Office. Thank you.

  • Closing

7.Father Ignacio Flores on behalf of Father Jose Alberto assistant Parish Priest and himself as Fr. In charge of Our Lady of Guadalupe Parish wishes all of you a blessed New Year.
May the Protection and Love of our Mother Mary of Guadalupe be with you all and bless each one of your families.

The family of Guadalupe Parish wants to be a strong, open, united, commited and mature christian community.
Participating actively in an integral growth and witnessing of christ
in upholding the integrity of creation
