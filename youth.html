Our Lady Of Guadalupe Parish-Youth

Youth Department

Defination

This a department within the pastrol ministry.It's designed to help young people identify and develop values that will enable them be in harmony with themselves and the community at large.Here they identify,nature their talents as they seek to identify God's will in their lives.

PMC Session

Defination

This is a session of faith formation in the youth department in the pastrol ministry.This session deals with formation of children of the age between 0 and 12 years old still instilling in the missionary spirit in them to take up their christian duty from a tender age and actively participate in the service of the church's members of the body of christ.It's here where the children put in to practice what they leran in catechesis, sunday school and PMC instructions through acts of charity and spirituality.

Objectives

a)To ensure that the childrenreceive spiritual formation at the grassroots through the decentralizing.
b)To empower youth/children to understand their role in the church and community.
c)To empower children/youth to take active partcipation in mass celebration and other parish activities.
d)To convene spiritual and social forums for youth/children.
e)To help young people understand their talents.

PMC Spirituality

a)Imitate the life of Jesus in simplicity.silence.his joyfulness and totoal openess and trust in His mother,docilty towards his Father and desire to dedicate himself to God his Father
b)The children should have missionary spirit through prayer and sacrifice

Aim of PMC

Is to foster christian faith and missionary formation of children and to help them take their responsibilities in the church as missionaries.

Symbols of PMC

i. To teach the catholic church faith and safeguard children's rights hence promoting their well-being.
ii. Help children play their role as christians inthe church of God.
iii. To help children be missionariesto other children(children helping children through their prayers,works of charity and welcoming others to join them.)
iv. To prepare children to become future Apostles e.g Priests, Religious brothers and sisters, catechists and responsible parents in the future.

CATEGORIES OF YOUTH

The youth are of the age of twelve years(12) to the age of thirty five years and are categorized into three main groups as follows:

a. Missionary Young Movement (MYM).

This is a group of youth from the age of twelve years to the age of seventeen years.They are termed as identity seekers.They seek to know who they are and als their identity in life basing on the cahnges of growth:
i. Physically
ii. Emotionally
iii. Sexually
iv. Mentally
This is a group who are in the adolescence stage of life. They are helped to identify themselves through formation and guidance so that they may live positively in reference to the challenges experienced in this age group.

b. Youth Serving Christ (YSC).

This is a group of youth from the age of eighteen years to twenty-five years.They are termed as the purpose seekers.They tend to look for a purpose in their life, thus, 'What should I choose to do in terms of vocation and career in life?'.They should choose well their vocation and career in life.The greatest challenge in this group relationships with the opposite sex, which they are guided and assisted through formation and counseling. Those who assist here are those who journey with the youth in their day-today-activities

c. Young Catholic Adults(YCA).

Is a group of adult youth from the age of twenty-six years up to the age of thirty-five.They are termed as the legacy seekers.They are the ones who ask themselves, 'What shall I leave behind in my life as a legacy of my life? '.In this stage they search to be satble all around their life.I t is their here that they make decision of who they want to be.They are assisted through formation and counseling to improve on it and live well.They are the providers, the security, caretakers and one who take responsibilities in the families and the society at the youthful stage.People depend on them on many issues, mature group of youth who are trained to be a role model to the younger ones.They are youth who take and make their own right decisions concerning life and how to live so that the others may follow.
